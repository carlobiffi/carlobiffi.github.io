Biography

I am a final-year PhD student in the Biomedical Image Analysis group at Imperial College London under the supervision of Prof Daniel Rueckert and Dr Declan O’Regan. My PhD work lies in the field of Machine Learning for Medical Image Analysis, particularly on the development of novel Deep Learning methodologies for the study of cardiovascular diseases. Currently, I am also working as a Computer Vision Research Intern at Huawei Noah’s Ark Lab in London, focussing on weakly supervised and few-shot object detection. My background is in Physics and I have a wide interest in Machine Learning applications to solve real-world problems, especially in the Medical Imaging domain.
